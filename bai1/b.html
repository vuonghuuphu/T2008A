NOUNS

A noun represents a peson, place, animal, thing or an idea.

KINDS OF NOUN

Nouns are classified as follows:


Proper:

It is the of a particular ofject, whether peson, place, animal, idea or thing

Common:

It is the name possessed by things in general.

Gollective:

It is the name representing a group of things.

Abstract:

It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions, conditions or actions.

PRONOUNS

A promoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noum.

KINDS OF PRONOUN.

Pronouns are classified as followws:

Personal pronouns:

It refer to a specific noun and used a substitute of the noun.

Interrogative pronouns:

It stands as the answer to the question about a person, place or a thing.

Indefinite pronouns:

It is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknown.Relative pronouns:

It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or expression preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statement.

Bemonstrative pronouns:

A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it.

Reflexive pronouns:

It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.

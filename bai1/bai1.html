nouns


A nouns represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

Kinds of noun

Nouns are classified as follows:

Proper: It is the name of a particular object, whether person, place, animal, ideaa or thing.

Common: It is the name possesed by things in general.

Collective:It is the name representhing a group of things.

Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotion, conditions or actions.


PRONOUNS

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.

KINDS OF PRONOUN

Pronouns are classified as follows:

Personal pronoun:It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun.

Interrogative pronoun: It stands as the answer to the question about person, place or a thing.

Indefinite pronouns:It is used to refer to person, thing or a place that are unknown.

Relative pronouns: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or expression preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.

Demonstrative pronouns A noun or pronoun is spectified and identified by it.

Reflexive pronouns: It usually folloows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
